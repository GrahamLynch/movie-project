Guess the Genre

This is a game that uses deep learning and neural networks to guess your favourite movie genre based on what types of movies you like and dislike.

Swipe RIGHT (--->) if you like a movie.

Swipe LEFT (---) if you dislike a movie.

When you are finished, the machine will recommend a movie genre for you based on your choices.

Enjoy!
